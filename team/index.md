# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-users" %}Team

The Intelligent Radio and Integrated Systems Group (IRIS) is led by [Prof. Ahmed Ben Ayed](/IRIS/members/Ahmed_Ben_Ayed.html) in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). The group is currently growing, and prospective students interested in high-frequency RF systems, antenna arrays, signal processing, and measurement science are encouraged to reach out.
