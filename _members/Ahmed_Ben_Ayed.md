Dr. Ahmed Ben Ayed is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where he leads the RF Systems Integration (RF-SI) Laboratory.

His research focuses on the design and experimental validation of high-frequency RF transceiver systems, with particular emphasis on mm-wave and sub-THz signal generation, beamforming antenna arrays, large-scale MIMO transmitters, and measurement-driven system characterization. His work adopts a system-level approach that tightly integrates RF circuits, digital signal processing, and measurement science to bridge the gap between theory and practical deployment.

Dr. Ben Ayed’s research has been disseminated through leading international conferences and archival journals in RF and microwave engineering, and is motivated by applications in next-generation wireless communications, satellite systems, and high-frequency sensing.
